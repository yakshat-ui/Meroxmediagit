Who We Are

Inside Merox

The vision and journey behind the agency.

Yakshat

Founder & Creative Director

I'm Yaksh. I've been working in this field since 2016. In 2019, I started Music Label "Difway Records" where I learned how content actually works and how artists grow. In 2022, I started "Behtareen Kalakaar" to support hip hop artists.

Since then, I've worked across brands, corporate, real estate, and advertising — handling cinematography, photography, and design. I don't like sticking to just one role and prefer being involved in the full process.

Work that looks good and actually works. That's what I'm building with Merox Media.

Live Now

Merox Media — Fully Launched

End-to-end media presence for premium brands. Production, digital strategy, and complete brand identity — all under one roof.

2016
The First Frame
Back in 2016, my cousin and I had no experience just a basic PC in a small room and a dream to make music. We tried our best on FL Studio, hoping to create a song where I would also handle the artwork. The song never happened, but it was the start of everything for us.
2017
First Clients & Real Work
By 2017, doing digital work became my only goal in life. With my family's support, I finally got my first camera, and it became an obsession. I couldn't go a single day without using it. I started doing local shoots with friends. My routine was fixed: shoot all day, come home in the evening, and edit. It wasn't just a hobby anymore; it was pure passion.
2018
Mastering The Craft
In 2018, with my family's support, I set up a music studio with dreams of starting a production house. I built a team, and though they aren’t around anymore, they helped me a lot. For a whole year, nothing worked out. I was great at the digital work, but I lacked a music producer. I tried learning the basics myself but really struggled to figure it out. Still, no matter how hard it got, I never lost hope.
2019
Post-Production + Difway
Finally, in 2019, I launched a proper music label named 'Difway Records' and dropped our first song. It was the best decision of my life! I built a great team who supported alot, we finally found a producer he supports alot, and new opportunities started rolling in. But honestly, the hustle only got harder. I was handling the entire label and all the digital work completely solo. There were a lot of ups and downs, but I never gave up. .
2020
Music Video Direction
Directed first professional music videos with a full crew. This is where a signature visual language started forming and I learned how to manage sets and execute creative visions.
2021
Commercial Ad Shoots
Stepped into commercial production. Started working with bigger brands, managing larger budgets, and delivering high-quality visual content on much tighter timelines.
2022
3D & VFX + Kalakaar
Unlocked 3D Modeling and VFX to push creative boundaries. Also launched 'Behtareen Kalakaar' to support and elevate independent hip-hop artists.
2023
Tech Meets Creativity
Started heavily blending cinematic visuals with technology. Focused on high-retention social content, fixing complex brand identities, and delivering complete digital growth packages.
2024
Full Creative Expansion
Scaled operations to manage digital identities, complex brand systems, and nationwide campaigns. It was no longer just about shooting; it was about building entire brands.
2025
The Foundation of Merox
The blueprint was finalized. Brought together a dedicated, core team of experts across applied arts, web development, business management, and music production to officially lay the groundwork for Merox Media.
