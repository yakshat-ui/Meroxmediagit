Inside Merox

The vision and journey behind the agency.

Yakshat

Founder & Creative Director

With over 9 years of experience behind the lens and the editing desk, my journey started from pure passion. I taught myself the craft by executing everything from raw brand shoots to stylized music videos.

I realized early on that modern brands need more than just one good video; they need an entire ecosystem of design, 3D aesthetics, and digital strategy. That’s why Merox Media was born.

Today, we operate as a full-house creative hub. The goal is simple: deliver youthful, out-of-the-box creativity with corporate-level trust and timelines.

2017

Bought the first camera. Started learning framing, lighting, and visual basics.

2019

Mastered advanced post-production, editing flows, and color grading.

2021

Directed professional music videos and stepped into commercial ad shoots.

2023

Expanded skills into VFX, 3D modeling, and built a dedicated creative team.

Today

Handling end-to-end media presence for brands, blending art with strategy.

Our Core Purpose

The Mission: To give brands a seamless, stress-free experience by providing top-tier production, 3D design, and digital strategy under one roof—no more juggling multiple freelancers.

The Vision: To become the most reliable and innovative creative agency for modern businesses, setting new standards for aesthetic storytelling.
