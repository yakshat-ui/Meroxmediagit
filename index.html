Inside Merox

The vision and journey behind the agency.

Yakshat

Founder & Creative Director

I'm Yaksh. I've been working in this field since 2016. In 2019, I started Music Label "Difway Records" where I learned how content actually works and how artists grow. In 2022, I started "Behtareen Kalakaar" to support hip hop artists.

Since then, I've worked across brands, corporate, real estate, and advertising handling cinematography, photography, and even design when needed. I don't like sticking to just one role and prefer being involved in the full process.

I keep things simple. Work that looks good and actually works. Now it's grown into a full creative setup — fresh work, done properly, on time, and something people can rely on. That's what I'm building with Merox Media.
